FAQs

Got a question? Our FAQs provide answers to common inquiries. For any queries not found here, please reach out via our contact form.

When are camping sites available and at what times are there fewer visitors on site?
At Waghinkars, camping sites are available almost every day upon advance booking. The volume of visitors fluctuates between weekdays and weekends.
What is the cost Camping gear?
Camping gear expenses are influenced by individual needs.
What are inclusions?
Tent stay, food, and sports activities are the basic inclusions at Waghinkars.
What are sport activities at camp?
Archery, cricket, football, volleyball, badminton are the outdoor sports activities along with dart game, carom, chess, ludo, variety of card games and many more indoor activities.
How are the tent arrangements?
We provide 2-person, 3-person, and 4-person tents as per request and group size. We will provide foam mattresses, bedsheets, pillows, and blankets.
What are the food options?

Snacks:

Tea, coffee, and pakoda.

Veg-BBQ:

Marinated paneer with assorted veggies.

NonVeg-BBQ:

Marinated chicken with mixed veggies.

Veg-dinner:

Paneer sabzi, any veg sabzi, chapati, dal-tadka, steamed rice, salad, sweets.

Nonveg-dinner:

Dry chicken or anda masala, chicken curry, chapati, steamed rice, salad, sweets.

Breakfast:

Tea, Poha, Anda bhurji, Bread, Jam, maggie.

How big are the tents. Are all amenities available during the rainy season?
We operate in the rain as well. We pitch the tent under the shade in the rainy season. Hiking is not permitted during rainy season.
Is charger point available at site or do we need to carry power bank?
Charger points available on every site at common area. Also charging points are available inside the cottages.
When is the best time for camping?

Spring (March to May):

Ideal for mountain regions with blooming flowers.

Autumn (October to November):

Popular after monsoon with clear skies.

Avoid Monsoon (July to September):

Heavy rainfall, landslides, slippery trails.

Always check local weather forecasts before planning your trip.

How far are the local market and petrol pump from Waghinkars?
The local market is 5 km away while HP petrol pump is 7.5 km away from camp.
What are the essential things to carry?
Carry odomos, extra clothes, slippers, comfortable footwear, warm clothing (winters), umbrella (monsoon).

Torch and mosquito repellent are available, extra blankets can be arranged.
How do I report something I've lost?
Fill our Lost and Found form, provide details about your lost item. Our team will provide updates upon locating your item.
